Hello, World

April 15, 2026

This is where I write about building. What I'm shipping, what I'm learning, what works and what doesn't.

I started this blog to document the journey. Not a polished narrative—just honest notes on iteration, shipping, and the things you learn by actually building things.

What This Is

A space for shipping stories. Posts about products I've built, patterns that work, mistakes I've made, and technical deep-dives on problems worth solving.

No fluff. No "10 ways to..." listicles. Just the craft of making things that work.

The Basics

I believe in:

  • Shipping fast. Done is better than perfect. Get it out, get feedback, iterate.
  • Writing clearly. If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough.
  • Learning openly. Building in public means learning in public. Both the wins and the failures.

Let's Build

If you're building something, I'd love to hear about it. Hit me up on GitHub.
